Bio

Mr. Rochana Obadage is a Computer Science PhD student in the Web Science and Digital Libraries (WS-DL) Group at Old Dominion University with a strong background in both academia and industry. His current research focuses on computational reproducibility and digital libraries. With experience as a Lecturer, he has refined his teaching and mentoring skills, guiding students through complex concepts and projects. Additionally, his role as a Machine Learning Engineer has provided him with practical expertise in designing and developing production-level microservices. He is passionate about advancing the field of computer science and contributing to innovative solutions through his research. His research interests include Digital Libraries, Computational Reproducibility, Computer Vision, Applied Machine Learning, and Science of Science. Mr. Rochana received his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, in 2021.
